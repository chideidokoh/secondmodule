How To Cook Rice

  1. Boil your water.
  2. Add any measure of rice you desire.
  3. Allow to perboil for 30minutes.
  4. Remove from fire, wash it and drain well.
  5. Put back on fire and allow boil for another 30minutes.
  6. Serve while hot.
